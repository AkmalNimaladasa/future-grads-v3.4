About Us

For over 23 years, we’ve nurtured children with care, love, and solid educational foundations. Founded in 2001 by Mrs. Yasmin Faiz, Future Grads continues to shape confident, kind, and curious learners.

What We Offer

Montessori (1.5 – 5 yrs)

Hands-on learning, independence & emotional growth guided by certified teachers.

Daycare (7am – 7pm)

Safe, loving, and flexible care for busy parents. Meals, play, naps, and learning.

International Grade 1 (2027)

English-medium, STEAM-rich curriculum launching in January 2027. Enroll early.

Vision & Mission

Vision: A joyful start to learning that inspires curiosity, values, and confidence.

Mission: Partnering with families to nurture lifelong learners and future-ready graduates.
